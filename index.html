About Me

Hi , I'm Saundarya , a web developer and designer based in Bhopal , India. I love creating beautiful and functional websites that help businesses grow and connect with their customers. I started my journey as a web developer last year, when I taught myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript through online courses and tutorials. Since then, I've worked on various projects, ranging from landing pages and blogs to e-commerce and social media platforms. I enjoy learning new technologies and frameworks, such as React, Bootstrap, and WordPress, to enhance my web development skills and deliver high-quality solutions. My goal is to help small and medium-sized businesses establish their online presence and reach their target audience. Whether you need a simple website, a complex web application, or a stunning logo, I can help you achieve your vision and objectives. In addition to web development and design, I'm also passionate about photography, music, dance and travel. I like to capture the beauty of nature and culture through my lens, sing and dance in my spare time , and explore new places and experiences whenever I can. If you're looking for a reliable, creative, and friendly web developer and designer, you've found her! I'd love to hear from you and discuss how we can work together on your next project. You can contact me via email, phone, or social media. You can also check out my portfolio below to see some of the websites I've created.
